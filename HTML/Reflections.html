Community Norms

Community norms are what most people in a community believe to be morally correct. The community norms at the hidden genius project seem to be fairly bacic. We do what is considered “good” by most people conforming to rules such as avoiding cursing and violence. I say considered good because community norms can be different depending on ones’ location.
